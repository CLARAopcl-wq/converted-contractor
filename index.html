Our Story

Pete Miller &
The Converted Contractor

📍 Locally Owned · Andover, OH · Since 2006

The Converted Contractor Construction was established in 2006 by Pete Miller after leaving the Amish community. With deep roots in traditional craftsmanship, Pete brings a level of skill and attention to detail that's rare in today's construction industry.

Over 20 years of hands-on experience means Pete understands construction from the ground up — literally. He and his professional team handle all phases of remodeling for any commercial or residential project in Ashtabula County, Ohio.

When you hire The Converted Contractor, you get a tradesman who takes pride in every nail, every beam, and every finish. Work done the right way, the first time.
